Pounded Yam

Yes it is! Yams are not only an excellent source of fibre but also high in potassium and manganese.

Pounded-yam is one of the many yam recipes in Nigeria. It is very delicious, one of the best Nigerian swallows in existence, and also very popular.

It is known as iyan by western Nigerian.


Ingredients

  • yam tuber
  • water

    • Steps

  1. Step 1

    Peel the skin off the yam and then slice.

  2. Step 2

    Put the sliced yam in the pot and boil till soft, add enough water so as the water in the yam won't dry up because we will need it while pounding.

  3. Step 3

    Scoop the hot yams into a mortar and pound with the pestle, if the yam that you are pouding gets stiff add some of the water used in cooking the yam into the mortar and keep pounding.

  4. Step 4

    Pound the yam until it has a stretchy consistency and doesn't stick when touched.

  5. Step 5

    Scoop your pounded yam in a plate.

  6. Step 6

    Serve with any Nigeria Soups for swallow recipes.
